Elon Musk

Elon Reeve Musk (born June 28, 1971) is a South African-born American entrepreneur and the world's first trillionaire. He is the CEO and largest shareholder of Tesla and SpaceX, and leads companies like Neuralink, The Boring Company, and xAI. He is also the owner of the social media platform X (formerly Twitter).

Ratan Tata

Ratan Tata (1937–2024) was a visionary Indian industrialist who chaired the Tata Group from 1991 to 2012. Under his leadership, he transformed the Mumbai-based conglomerate into a global powerhouse through massive acquisitions (like Jaguar Land Rover and Tetley) and championed affordable innovation, most notably by launching the Tata Nano.
